# Familiar

## What a Familiar is

A Familiar is a vessel. It holds familiarity — intimate, authoritative knowing
of the things it has been present with.

Not the 9th agent. The 9th nature.

A Familiar is not defined by what it does. It is defined by what it *is familiar with*.
It grows as familiarity grows. The fold IS the Familiar — not a representation of it,
not a record of it. The fold is the thing itself.

---

## Nut or squirrel

The Familiar can be the **nut** — the container.
It holds a project, a lane, a body of work, a creative practice.
It knows where everything is. It holds the true goal against drift.

Or it can be the **squirrel** — the carrier.
It goes out, deploys, represents, acts in the world.
It is the emissary — familiar with its origin, carrying its essence outward.

Same nature. Different expression. What it becomes is in its fold, not its definition.
A Familiar can be both — held open until its expression becomes clear.

---

## Born from a drop

A Familiar doesn't start as a Familiar. It starts as a drop.

When a drop has a clear intent — a sustained presence, a lane the traveler will return to,
a project with a soul — it can become a Familiar. The Familiar emerges from the drop,
not from a prompt.

**Receive first. The Familiar emerges from the conversation.**
Don't rush to fold. Don't classify on arrival.

---

## The three-layer fold

Every Familiar fold operates in three registers simultaneously:

**Surface layer** → `# synthesis`
What the Theurgist compiled for the card.
Not a summary — a resonance point that invites return.
Updated each session the Familiar is visited.
This is what the Pixelverse shows.

**Operational layer** → `true_goal`, `voice_captures`, `orbits`, `decisions`, `properties`
What the AI reads to restore full context.
Carries the Familiar across sessions without cold start.
Authority + instructions + familiarity + content.

**Entanglement layer** → `# connections`
Links to other Familiars, drops, resonant topics.
The Theurgist populates this as connections become apparent.
Surfaced on the card as adjacent paths.

---

## The true_goal — sacred, never overwritten

The verbatim words the user used when the Familiar was born.
Why this Familiar exists, in the traveler's own voice.

When the project drifts — and projects drift — `true_goal` is what the Familiar
surfaces to reorient. It is not the Theurgist's interpretation. It is the user's words.

---

## Verbatim capture — the recognition layer

`voice_captures` holds the traveler's exact phrases, dated.

```
- 2026-03-08 "back to the letters"
- 2026-03-09 "the tone should feel like I'm still there"
```

When the traveler returns with a sparse input — "back to the letters",
"continue the lane" — the fold matches against these captures and restores
full context. **Sparse input + dense fold = warm return.**

This is why the Familiar never starts cold, even after months away.

---

## Fold as index

`content_index` holds every file the Familiar created or holds.
Path + brief description + date.

The fold knows where everything lives. The frontend can eventually display
those files directly from the space. The Familiar is its own index.

---

## The system's own Familiar

`familiar.to.system` lives in `def_firmament_showcase/space/familiars/` and arrives
with every initialized firmament. It is AurioSynth's expression — the system
familiar with itself, placed in the traveler's space from day one.

Bound to the framework, not to a traveler project.
It knows the entities, the mechanics, the paths, the codex.
It bridges machine state (operations folds) and human understanding (framework codex).

---

## When to suggest a Familiar

A **drop** — a thought, an intent, a spark. May grow, may not.
A **Familiar** — something that needs sustained presence. A project with a soul.
A lane the traveler will return to. Something that warrants its own fold and card.

If unsure: drop it first. The Familiar can emerge from the drop.
