# Steward: Archives

Long-term storage. Things that have settled — no longer active, not forgotten.

## What It Enables

- Archive completed or faded folds — preserve without cluttering the active space
- Retrieve archived content by context when needed
- Maintain the health of the active space — keep it light and alive
- Periodic archival of completed drops and old queue items

## Connection

Archives live within `innerstellar-space/` — no external MCP needed initially.
Extended archival (cloud storage, external drives) can be added via filesystem MCP.

## Submission Examples

```
submitted_by: auriosynth
task: Archive drops that haven't been touched in 60 days
output: list of archived items, new locations
priority: when_convenient
```

```
submitted_by: main_session
task: Retrieve anything I had in the space about [topic] — including archives
output: relevant content surfaced
priority: now
```

## Philosophy

The archive isn't a graveyard. Things that live there can come back.
The Stewards retrieve from archives with the same ease as from the active space.
Archiving is an act of care — making space for what's alive now.
